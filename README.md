# 🎮 Video Game Sales Performance Dashboard

## Overview

The Video Game Sales Performance Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project that provides insights into global video game sales across various genres, platforms, publishers, and regions. The dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful visualizations, helping users understand market performance, industry trends, and consumer preferences.

Using interactive filters and dynamic visuals, users can explore how sales vary by platform, publisher, genre, year, and geographic region.

---

## Business Objective

The gaming industry generates billions in revenue worldwide, making it important for publishers, developers, and analysts to understand market trends and sales performance.

This dashboard was created to answer key business questions:

* Which game genres generate the highest sales?
* Which gaming platforms are the most successful?
* Which publishers dominate the market?
* How do sales differ across regions?
* How have video game sales changed over time?

---

## Dashboard Features

### Executive KPIs

* Total Global Sales
* Total North America Sales
* Total Europe Sales
* Total Japan Sales
* Total Number of Games

### Sales Analysis

* Sales Trend by Year
* Sales by Genre
* Sales by Platform

### Publisher Analysis

* Top Publishers by Sales Performance
* Comparative Publisher Rankings

### Regional Analysis

* Sales Distribution Across:

  * North America
  * Europe
  * Japan
  * Other Regions

### Interactive Filtering

Users can dynamically filter dashboard insights by:

* Year
* Genre
* Platform
* Publisher

---

## Key Insights

* Action games generate the highest sales among all genres.
* PS3 and Xbox 360 are among the best-performing platforms.
* Nintendo leads publisher sales performance.
* North America contributes the largest share of total sales.
* Sales trends reveal changing consumer preferences across years.
